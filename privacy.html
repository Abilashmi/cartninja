At The Cart Ninja, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and Shopify application.

01

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

 a. Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact details

 b. Store & Usage Data (for Shopify merchants)

  • Store name and URL
  • Installed app data
  • Cart interactions and usage behavior
  • Analytics related to app performance

 c. Technical Data

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Cookies and tracking technologies
02

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate our services
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Analyze performance and usage trends
  • Communicate updates, support and important notices
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
03

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only in the following cases:

  • With Shopify, as required for app functionality
  • With trusted third-party service providers (analytics, hosting)
  • When required by law or legal processes
04

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enhance user experience
  • Track usage and performance
  • Store preferences

 You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

05

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

06

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations.

07

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

Access your personal data
Request correction
Request deletion
Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us:

support@thecartninja.com We typically respond within 1–2 business days
08

Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with third-party tools (like Shopify). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you use in connection with our app.

09

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
